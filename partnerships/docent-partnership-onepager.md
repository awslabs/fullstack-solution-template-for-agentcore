# Docent — Museum & Gallery Partnership

---

## What is Docent?

Docent is a free iOS app and website that helps people discover museums, galleries, and cultural exhibitions. Users follow curated tours, write reviews, and share their visits with friends — building a social layer around the museum experience.

**Based in San Francisco** · **iOS App Store** · **docentofficial.com**

---

## What Partners Get

| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Featured Placement** | Priority listing in the app and website with rich venue profiles |
| **Exhibit Spotlights** | Dedicated blog posts and social media features for current and upcoming exhibitions |
| **Direct Ticket Links** | In-app links to your ticketing page, driving conversions from engaged visitors |
| **Visitor Analytics** | Aggregated data on saves, tour completions, reviews, and visitor interest trends |

---

## Enhanced Visitor Reviews

Docent reviews go beyond star ratings. Our editorial-format review system gives your venue richer, more actionable visitor feedback:

- **Magazine-quality presentation** — serif typography, drop caps, and pull quotes make reviews feel like journal entries, not Yelp posts
- **Rich media** — visitors embed photos and video walkthroughs directly in their reviews, creating authentic visual content for your exhibitions
- **Social discovery** — users follow friends, see their reviews first, and share visits — turning every review into organic word-of-mouth
- **Deeper signal** — longer, more thoughtful reviews give your team real insight into what resonates with visitors
